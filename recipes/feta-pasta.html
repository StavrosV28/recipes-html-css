How to make Feta Pasta

Back to HomePage

Feta pasta is a simple, creamy pasta dish made by roasting a block of feta cheese with cherry tomatoes, garlic, and olive oil until the tomatoes burst and the feta melts. The roasted ingredients are then mashed and mixed with cooked pasta, often including additions like fresh basil, creating a flavorful and tangy sauce that coats the pasta.

Ingredients

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Pasta(preferably a rotini or cellentani)
  • Crushed red pepper
  • Garlic
  • Basil (optional)

Steps:

  1. Get started by preheating your oven to 400Â°. While it's heating up, mince your garlic and anything else you wish to add to the dish. After preparations have been made, place a block of feta cheese in the middle of the baking dish. Then place all your cherry tomatoes in the dish spreading them around evenly.
  2. Season all the ingredients in the dish with a little bit of salt, pepper, oregano, and crushed red pepper.
  3. Once the oven is preheated, place the baking dish inside and bake until the feta is golden. Typically this may take anywhere between 30-45 minutes.
  4. Once the feta is golden brown, take it out of the oven and put your ready made pasta in the baking dish. Now mix everything together coating all the pasta with the feta/tomatoes.
  5. Garnish with basil and enjoy!
